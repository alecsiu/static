💡 Reading the stack

0% LTCG room is the remaining taxable-income space under the 0% qualified-dividend / long-term-capital-gain threshold. Ordinary taxable income uses that space first, so QD/LTCG only get 0% treatment for the portion that still fits below the threshold.

Deductions are shown as a blue shield. The taxable row then begins with ordinary-income bracket layers, followed by QD/LTCG layers. Purple basis is cash-flow support, not taxable income.

Educational federal tax visualization only. Does not include state tax, AMT, NIIT, ACA subsidy effects, IRMAA, passive activity loss limits, QBI, depreciation recapture, credits, or phaseouts.
